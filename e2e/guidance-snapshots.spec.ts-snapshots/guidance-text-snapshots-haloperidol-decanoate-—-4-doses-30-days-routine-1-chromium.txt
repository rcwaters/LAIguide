Next steps:

(1) Administer usual haloperidol decanoate injection.

(2) Arrange for next injection in 4 weeks.

When to notify provider:

Albumin is < 3.0 or total bilirubin is > 1.5 and if this is not known to the prescriber

New side effects from recent injection

New abnormal involuntary movements, excessive sedation, dizziness, agitation, delirium, fever, tachycardia, abnormal blood pressure, or any concerning symptoms
