Administer the usual Aristada dose as soon as possible, then assess the need for supplementation.

---

Recommended supplementation:

Oral aripiprazole for 7 days OR Aristada Initio 675 mg IM once (now or within next few days)

---

When to notify provider:

Notify provider with patient preference for supplementation and to order medications

New side effects from recent injection

New abnormal involuntary movements, excessive sedation, dizziness, agitation, delirium, fever, tachycardia, abnormal blood pressure, or any concerning symptoms
