Next steps:

Administer usual fluphenazine decanoate injection if the dose is 50 mg or less. (If the dose is greater than 50 mg, consult provider before administering.)

If symptoms of psychosis have returned or worsened, recommend oral fluphenazine supplementation (consult provider for order).

Schedule a check-in with patient in approximately 24 hours (near peak dose) to assess for fluphenazine-related adverse effects.

---

When to notify provider:

Pre-injection: Consult provider only if fluphenazine decanoate dose is greater than 50 mg.

Post-injection: Notify provider of the situation and request an order for oral supplementation if needed (returned or worsened psychosis symptoms).

New side effects from recent injection

New abnormal involuntary movements, excessive sedation, dizziness, agitation, delirium, fever, tachycardia, abnormal blood pressure, or any concerning symptoms
